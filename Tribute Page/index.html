Diana, Princess of Wales(1961-1997)

The royal family member who changed millions of lives throught her charitable work

Diana poses with Prince Charles and her sons, Prince William and Prince Harry, in the Royal Family Portrait of 1986.

Here is a timeline of Princess Diana's life:

  • 1961 - Born in Park House, Sandringham

  • 1969 - At the age of 7, parents get divorced and remarry; her father to Raine Spencer, Countess of Dartmoor, and her mother to Peter Shand Kydd

  • 1970 - Enrolls at Riddlesworth Hall, an all-girls boarding school in Norfolk at the age of 9

  • 1973 - Enrolls in West Heath Girls' School in Kent and graduates without a diploma

  • 1975 -Inherits the title "Lady" after her father becomes the Earl Spencer

  • 1977 - Diana, at the age of 16, meets Prince Charles for the first time

  • February 1981 - Diana and Charles announce their engagement

  • March 1981 - Diana attends her first ever post-engagement social event

  • July 1981 - Diana gets married to Prince Charles at St. Pauls's Cathedral

  • November 1981 - Diana gets pregnant with her first child

  • June 1982 - Prince William is born at St. Mary's Hospital and is given the name William Arthur Phillip Louis

  • 1983 - Diana leaves for her first ever royal tour to Australia and New Zealand

  • February 1984 - Diana gets pregnant with her second child

  • September 1984 - Prince Henry(Harry) is born and is named Henry Charles Albert David

  • 1989 - Diana discovers that Prince Charles was secretly having an affair with Camilla Parker Bowles

  • March 1992 - Diana's father, John Spencer dies of a heart attack

  • December 1992 - Diana and Charles part ways after 11 years of marriage

  • 1995 - Diana makes national appearance and discusses the effects of the divorce on her such as self-harm and bulimia

  • 1996 - Prince Charles and Diana's divorce is confirmed and Diana retians the title, "The Princess of Wales", although she loses any sort of future claims to the throne

  • August 1997 - After a trip to Sardina with Dodi Fayed and leaving in a limousine post dinner at the Ritz-Carlton Hotel, Diana is pursued by the paparazzi, which then results in a crash at the Pont de l'Alma tunnel. Although Diana's bodyguard survives, she suffers severe injury and is pronounced dead shortly afterwards at the Pltie-St.Salpetriere Hospital at the age of 36.

  • September 1997 - Diana's funeral is held and is buried at her family estate in Althorp. An estimated 2.5 billion people worldwide tuned in on televeision and more than a million lined up on the streets of London to pay their respects.
